I’m a Computer Science PhD student at [Brock University](https://brocku.ca/) and a Graduate Research Assistant in the Nature-Inspired Computational Intelligence Lab.

My research develops **multi-objective optimization**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"} methods for machine learning, with applications in multi-task and single-task learning, multi-objective learning, memory compression, and responsible AI. I also study **dimensionality reduction in optimization**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"} and **subspace optimization for efficient fine-tuning**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}.

I’m particularly interested in problems where **learning, optimization, and model efficiency**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"} meet, including multi-task learning, representation learning, machine unlearning, and neural network compression.

Previously, I completed my MASc. in Computer Engineering at [Ontario Tech University](https://ontariotechu.ca/), where I worked on center-based sampling, large-scale global optimization, differential evolution, and multi-objective optimization.

I have published in CEC, SSCI, SMC, CCECE, Swarm and Evolutionary Computation, Scientific Reports, and Canadian AI. I also build research software for optimization benchmarks, neural network training, and reproducible academic workflows.

Alongside research, I enjoy teaching, mentoring, and course design. I have served as an **instructor**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"} for **Topics in Computational Intelligence**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"} and have assisted courses in **artificial intelligence**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}, **data structures**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}, **machine learning**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}, **engineering design**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}, and **software engineering**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}.

Brock students interested in undergraduate research projects, including **COSC 3P99**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"} or **COSC 4F90**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}, with me and [Prof. Beatrice Ombuki-Berman](https://brocku.ca/mathematics-science/computer-science/faculty-research/faculty-directory/beatrice-ombuki-berman/) are welcome to reach out.
