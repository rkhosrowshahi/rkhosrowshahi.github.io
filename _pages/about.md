I’m a Computer Science PhD student at [Brock University](https://brocku.ca/) working under supervision of Prof. Shahryar Rahnamayan and Prof. Beatrice Ombuki-Berman.

My thesis research focuses on **gradient-free optimization and learning**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"} for memory compression and other challenging machine learning problems, especially when gradients are unavailable, expensive, or difficult to use effectively. I use **gradient-free evolutionary algorithms**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"} with **subspace optimization for efficient fine-tuning**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}, working at the intersection of **learning, optimization, and model efficiency**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}. More broadly, my interests include multi-task and single-task learning, multi-objective learning, representation learning, machine unlearning, responsible AI, and **dimensionality reduction in optimization**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}.

Previously, I completed my MASc. in Computer Engineering at [Ontario Tech University](https://ontariotechu.ca/), where I worked on center-based sampling, large-scale global optimization, differential evolution, and multi-objective optimization.

I also work on **gradient-based multi-objective learning**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"} through research software. I build open-source ML infrastructure for scalable experimentation, PyTorch training workflows, and reproducible optimization research, and I am currently contributing to the [**TorchJD framework**](https://torchjd.org/stable/){: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}, an [open-source](https://github.com/SimplexLab/TorchJD) PyTorch library for Jacobian descent and multi-objective optimization.

Alongside research, I enjoy teaching, mentoring, and course design. I have served as an **instructor**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"} for **Topics in Computational Intelligence**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"} and have assisted courses in **artificial intelligence**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}, **data structures**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}, **machine learning**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}, **engineering design**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}, and **software engineering**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}.

Brock students interested in undergraduate research projects, including **COSC 3P99**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"} or **COSC 4F90**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}, with me and [Prof. Beatrice Ombuki-Berman](https://brocku.ca/mathematics-science/computer-science/faculty-research/faculty-directory/beatrice-ombuki-berman/) are welcome to reach out.
