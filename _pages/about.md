I’m a **Computer Science PhD student** at [Brock University](https://brocku.ca/){: style="color: var(--global-theme-color); text-decoration: underline; text-underline-offset: 0.12em;"}![Brock University logo](/assets/img/logos/brock-small.png){: style="height: 0.8em; width: auto; margin-left: 0.2em; vertical-align: -0.08em;"} working under supervision of Prof. [Shahryar Rahnamayan](https://rahnamayan.ca/){: style="color: var(--global-theme-color); text-decoration: underline; text-underline-offset: 0.12em;"} and Prof. [Beatrice Ombuki-Berman](https://www.ombuki-berman.com/){: style="color: var(--global-theme-color); text-decoration: underline; text-underline-offset: 0.12em;"}.

My research focuses on **gradient-free optimization and learning** for **memory compression** and other challenging machine learning problems, especially when gradients are unavailable, expensive, or difficult to use effectively. I use gradient-free evolutionary algorithms with **subspace optimization** for efficient fine-tuning, working at the intersection of learning, optimization, and **model efficiency**. More broadly, my interests include multi-task and single-task learning, multi-objective learning, representation learning, machine unlearning, responsible AI, and dimensionality reduction in optimization.

Previously, I completed my **MASc. in Computer Engineering** at [Ontario Tech University](https://ontariotechu.ca/){: style="color: var(--global-theme-color); text-decoration: underline; text-underline-offset: 0.12em;"} ![Ontario Tech University logo](/assets/img/logos/otu-small.png){: style="height: 0.8em; width: auto; margin-left: 0.2em; vertical-align: -0.08em;"}, where I worked on center-based sampling, large-scale global optimization, differential evolution, and multi-objective optimization.

I also work on **gradient-based multi-objective learning** through research software. I build open-source ML infrastructure for scalable experimentation, PyTorch training workflows, and reproducible optimization research, and I am currently contributing to the [**TorchJD**{: style="color: var(--global-theme-color);"}](https://torchjd.org/stable/){: style="color: var(--global-theme-color); text-decoration: underline; text-underline-offset: 0.12em;"} framework, an [open-source](https://github.com/SimplexLab/TorchJD){: style="color: var(--global-theme-color); text-decoration: underline; text-underline-offset: 0.12em;"} PyTorch library for Jacobian descent and multi-objective optimization.

Alongside research, I enjoy teaching, mentoring, and course design. I taught **Topics in Computational Intelligence ([COSC 4P96](https://calendar.brocku.ca/preview_course_nopop.php?catoid=23&coid=88513){: style="color: var(--global-theme-color); text-decoration: underline; text-underline-offset: 0.12em;"})** at Brock University and have assisted courses in artificial intelligence, data structures, machine learning, engineering design, and software engineering.

Brock students interested in undergraduate research projects, including **[COSC 3P99](https://brocku.ca/mathematics-science/computer-science/programs/course-list/cosc-3p99-4f90/){: style="color: var(--global-theme-color); text-decoration: underline; text-underline-offset: 0.12em;"}** or **[COSC 4F90](https://brocku.ca/mathematics-science/computer-science/programs/course-list/cosc-3p99-4f90/){: style="color: var(--global-theme-color); text-decoration: underline; text-underline-offset: 0.12em;"}**, with me and Prof. [Beatrice Ombuki-Berman](https://www.ombuki-berman.com/){: style="color: var(--global-theme-color); text-decoration: underline; text-underline-offset: 0.12em;"} are welcome to reach out.
